Two hundred seventy-one posts. That would translate to one thousand four hundred thirty-two
pages or three hundred thirty-six thousand seven hundred eighty-one words. That's the amount 
of content I've written on this little blog of mine. The most impressive number, though, 
is the hundreds of thousands of people who, throughout the years, read all these words 
and were inspired by them.

Some words were more popular than others; some words were wrong and had to be corrected; 
some words were technical, others emotional. All words were handwritten by me whenever 
I felt inspired to write them.

In a time when handwritten things seem to lose value, replaced by unreliable copies of copies; 
I wanted a permanent memory of my written words. I made a selection of less than half of them, 
printed in this book — something I can physically hold and know it's real.

Enjoy.
